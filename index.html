COE Tactical View — Why No Move Yet

Cat B closed May Bid 2 at $129,501 — 1,469 bids for 869 quotas (1.69× oversubscribed). Despite a 7% quota increase for May–Jul, prices keep rising. The reason: dealers at the May 9–10 expo sold 6–8 attempt guaranteed COE packages to customers. Each package = committed bidding demand for 6–8 consecutive rounds regardless of price.
